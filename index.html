About Me

Enoch Wu is a long-lost native of San Francisco — born on the Bay and raised in the heart of the Rust Belt that is the Midwest. He is a man of many talents — on the search for a way to synthesize all that he knows into unique solutions. For him, the journey is one where technology and the humanities cross paths infinately. He is an appreciator of good design; in addition to the free flow and accessibility of information.

At the end of the day, he admires the simple life — kicking back, performing Irish tunes with musicians, or catching up with friends over food drink, board games, and PC matches.
